justification of task
ans 1 - I chose the Ripple Edit Tool because the task required removing or adjusting a clip without leaving empty gaps in the timeline. If I used the basic Selection Tool and moved clips manually, I would have needed multiple steps: delete the clip, select the remaining clips, drag them forward, and then recheck sync and timing.

With the Ripple Edit Tool, the timeline automatically closed the gap after trimming or deleting the section. This made the workflow much faster and cleaner.


ans - 2 
 my approach was to organize the footage clearly before starting detailed editing. I used Label Colors to separate different types of clips such as original footage, edited clips, B-roll, graphics, and backup versions. This made the timeline easier to understand and helped me work faster during the edit.

Using Label Colors also protects the project when a client requests major changes because the original footage remains easy to identify at any time. Instead of replacing or confusing edited clips with raw files, I can quickly locate the untouched source footage and create new edits without damaging the existing work.


ans - 3
Applying Title Safe Zones helped ensure that all text, captions, and graphics stayed within the visible and readable area of the screen on different devices and platforms. For a lifestyle influencer, professionalism is very important because the content represents their personal brand and audience experience.
